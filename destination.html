Exploring the Wonders of Puerto Princesa, Philippines – A Tourist’s Paradise

Top sight | Puerto Princesa, Palawan

Puerto Princesa Subterranean River

The Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park is a must-visit attraction in Palawan island. It boasts the world’s longest underground river, spanning over 8.2 kilometers and surrounded by stunning limestone karst mountain formations.

Puerto Princesa: Swim with Whale Sharks Boat Cruise

Discover the whale sharks, that are called “Butanding” in Filipino! This peaceful and gentle giant is cruising between the months of April to October in the distant part of Honda Bay area and can be seen around one week before or after each full moons. With our activity, we bring you there to experience this encounter with the gentle-giants “Butanding”!

Puerto Princesa:Honda Bay Island Hopping

Honda Bay is the place to be when looking for some fun in the sun, sand, and sea in Palawan. Kick off your adventure with a convenient hotel pick up service within Puerto Princesa. Once you arrive at the Honda Bay wharf, you’ll be greeted by your friendly English-speaking guide and tour operator. Hop aboard a motorized boat and head off for Luli Island. Play along the long, flag-decorated sandbar and enjoy the calm waves on the shore!

Puerto Princesa: Half-Day City Tour

Become more familiar with Puerto Princesa and its main hangouts and highlights on this 5-hour tour, including round-trip transport from your city hotel. Along your tour route, visit eight destinations, including Plaza Cuartel, Immaculate Conception Cathedral, Baylwalk, Crocodile Farm, Baker's Hill, Pasalubong Center, and Binuatan Creations Weaving Center..

Puerto Princesa: Jungle Firefly Watching Boat Tour

The fireflies of Puerto Princesa are famous for being a breathtaking spectacle. This tour will get you right up close to this amazing natural wonder. You'll be able to enjoy a delicious buffet dinner before you climb aboard a boat and set off on an underground river. As dusk falls and darkness settles, the light show of the fireflies begin. It is only in Southeast Asia where fireflies glow in unison

Nagtabon Beach

Nagtabon Beach is a beautiful tropical paradise located approximately 45 minutes from Puerto Princesa City in the Philippines. This stunning beach with crystal-clear waters and soft sand, is surrounded by lush greenery, including coconut palms and other tropical trees. The serenity of the beach is enhanced by the fact that it is still relatively unknown to tourists, allowing visitors to experience a peaceful and secluded atmosphere.

Puerto Princesa: Zipline, Paddleboat Tour

Explore the Underground River, ride a zipline over the sea, and enjoy a paddleboat tour through a mangrove forest on a full-day tour in Sabang.

Puerto Princesa : Ugong Rock

Go on a day of exploration and thrilling adventures with this tour of Puerto Princesa's Ugong Rock, which takes you rappelling, spelunking and more! Experience a morning full of adrenaline rush as you explore and climb Ugong Rock, a massive moss-covered limestone formation with a gorgeous view of the mountains and the surrounding rice fields at the summit. You'll see beautiful untouched mineral deposits in the caves

Tubbataha Reef National Marine Park

The Tubbataha Natural Park, also known as the Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park, is a protected area of the Philippines located in the middle of the Sulu Sea. The marine and bird sanctuary consists of two huge atolls and the smaller Jessie Beazley Reef covering a total area of 97,030 hectares

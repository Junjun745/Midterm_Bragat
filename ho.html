Puerto Princesa City, a tropical paradise with a bustling urban center perfect for business with leisure trips. Lush rainforests and white sand beaches are simply hours away from the city proper where a banking and commercial boom has made life easier for workaholics who can never run away from the office.Puerto Princesa City is a relatively young settlement. It was established as a city only in 1970.The first settlers arrived here almost a century earlier though, on March 4, 1872, celebrated every year hence as the foundation day of Puerto Princesa.
